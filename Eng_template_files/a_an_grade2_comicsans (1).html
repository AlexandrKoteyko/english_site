🛒 Sam's Lunch — Обід Сема
Hello! My name is Sam. I am seven years old.
Today I have an apple and a banana in my bag.
Mum gave me an egg sandwich and a bottle of water.
For dessert I want an ice cream or a cookie.
My friend Lucy has a carrot, an orange
and an avocado. She loves healthy food!
I also see a cake on the table. Yummy! 🎂
A — banana, bottle, cookie, carrot, cake
AN — apple, egg, ice cream, orange, avocado

Привіт! Мене звати Сем. Мені сім років.
