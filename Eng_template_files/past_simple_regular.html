⚡ Past Simple ⚡

Regular Verbs · +ed / +d / doubled

📚 Rules: How to form Past Simple?
RuleWhen?Base formPast Simple
+ edMost verbsplay   watch   visitplayed   watched   visited
+ dEnds in -elive   like   dancelived   liked   danced
y → iedconsonant + ystudy   try   carrystudied   tried   carried
double + edCVC short vowelstop   plan   dropstopped   planned   dropped
💡 Sentence structure:   Subject + verb+ed + ...     ✅ She played football.   ❌ She play football.
❗ Negative: Subject + didn't + base verb   →   She didn't play football.
❓ Question: Did + subject + base verb?   →   Did she play football?
